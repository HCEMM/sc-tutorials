## ğŸ—“ï¸ How to Book a Reservation

Our system is built around a central booking calendar.

1.  Navigate to the **"Calendar"** page.
2.  **Select Your Microscope:** Use the filter to choose the microscope you wish to reserve.
3.  **View Availability:** The calendar will display all booked (gray) and available (white) time slots. Use the arrows to navigate between different days or weeks. Select the exact date to list available time slots.
4.  **Choose Your Slot:** Click on an **available** time slot.
5.  **Confirm Details:** The selected date and time slot appears.
    * Please verify the **date, time, and instrument**.
    * Click **"Confirm Appointment"**.
6.  Your reservation is now complete! It will immediately appear in your personal appointments list.

## ğŸ“‹ Managing Your Appointments,

You can easily view all your upcoming reservations or cancel them if your plans change.

### How to See Your Appointments

1.  Navigate to the **"Reservations"** page.
2.  This page will provide a complete list of all your upcoming and past reservations, sorted by date.

### How to Delete a Reservation

1.  Go to your **"My Reservations"** page.
2.  Find the specific reservation you wish to cancel from the list.
3.  Click the **"Delete"** button next to the appointment.
4.  A prompt will ask you to confirm the cancellation. Click **"Yes"** to confirm.

> **Important:** Please be courteous to other users. Cancel your reservations as far in advance as possible to free up the time slot. `[Terms and Conditions 3.2. "Reservations must be canceled no later than 24 hours before the scheduled time if the user cannot attend."]`
